### Why These Projects?

Industrial supply chain is one of the least digitized sectors in the economy. Companies still process RFQs by hand, match suppliers from memory, and learn about disruptions after they've already impacted production.

These tools apply AI where it creates the most value — not in consumer apps, but in the complex, document-heavy workflows that run $600B+ in industrial distribution every year.
