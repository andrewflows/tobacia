How Pathfinder works — Architecture & Technical Deep-Dive

The Problem

When a procurement team needs to source a specific part — say a 10" trunnion-mounted ball valve in Class 600 with NACE compliance — they rely on institutional memory, outdated catalogs, or hours of manual research. The knowledge of which manufacturer makes the best fit for a given spec lives in the heads of experienced buyers, not in searchable systems.

The Solution

Pathfinder uses AI with deep industrial domain knowledge to analyze a part specification and return ranked supplier recommendations. It understands pressure classes, material grades, API standards, and manufacturer product lines — the kind of knowledge that typically takes years to accumulate.

About

Built by Andres Tobacia — Industrial Engineer with 17+ years in manufacturing, supply chain, and space exploration.

No data is stored. All processing is stateless. AI processing powered by a large language model API. No training is performed on input data.

These tools are portfolio demonstrations built to showcase AI-driven approaches to common industrial problems. They are not commercial products — outputs may not reflect current pricing, availability, or specifications, and should not be used as the basis for procurement or sourcing decisions. No user data is stored.
