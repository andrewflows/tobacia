Sermon-to-Note ✝️

Paste a sermon transcript (or your notes from listening). AI extracts theology key points + scripture references + 3 application questions for your Obsidian Vault/Andres/Faith/ folder.

💡 To get a transcript from YouTube: open the video → click "..." below it → Show transcript → copy the text. Or just paste your own listening notes.
