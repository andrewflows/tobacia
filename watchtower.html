How Watchtower works — Architecture & Technical Deep-Dive

The Problem

Supply chain disruptions cost manufacturers billions every year. A port closure in Rotterdam, a tariff change on Chinese steel, a capacity crunch at a key foundry — these events cascade through supply chains before most procurement teams even hear about them. By the time you know, it's too late to pivot.

The Solution

Watchtower analyzes your supplier portfolio against current geopolitical, logistics, trade, and capacity risks. It identifies vulnerabilities, assesses probability and impact, and recommends specific mitigation actions — before disruptions hit your production line.

About

Built by Andres Tobacia — Industrial Engineer with 17+ years in manufacturing, supply chain, and space exploration.

No data is stored. All processing is stateless. AI processing powered by a large language model API. No training is performed on input data.

These tools are portfolio demonstrations built to showcase AI-driven approaches to common industrial problems. They are not commercial products — outputs may not reflect current pricing, availability, or specifications, and should not be used as the basis for procurement or sourcing decisions. No user data is stored.
